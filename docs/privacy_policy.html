VigilTip is designed with privacy as our top priority. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

Your data security is our priority:

  • No Server Access: We have no access to your receipt images or calculations

6. Your Rights

  • You can disable optional analytics in settings
  • No data retention policies apply since we don't store your data
